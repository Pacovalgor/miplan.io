Use technology to live in the Real Reality

Welcome to miplan


Currently, social networks have made us increasingly relate to more people, but only in the digital world. We want all these relationships translated into moments in the real world. Have the peace of mind that if you have to go to a new city, you have plans to do it from the first moment. The tranquility that there are many people in the world who have your tastes, and do not have to be from your circle nearby.
 We want you to enjoy your time to the fullest, and for this we have created miplan.

Create a plan

If you want to talk about basketball while you have a coffee, but in your circle of friends you are the only one who likes basketball. If you are passionate about table games but you lack people to play with. If you want to meet people in your new city who also share your interests with you



Find a plan

if you have free time and do not know what to do. If the plan of your friends do not like you very much but you do not feel like staying at home either. Find a plan!
miplan will recommend you plans in your city depending on your interests.



Enjoy the city

you are interested in a particular topic, but your friends do not share that interest. Make new contacts with people who like the same things as you. miplan will recommend you people within your city or the city that you choose ordered by the amount of interests you have in common.




Talk with your new friends

have you found a plan that you like? sign up !! As soon as they accept you, you will have a new chat available in which you can talk with your new friends, this chat will disappear as soon as the plan is finalized.
